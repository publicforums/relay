Features

Everything you need to have real and focused conversations.

Relay is made to be simple and easy to use. It gives you the main features you need so you can talk, share, and stay connected without it feeling complicated.

Public chat

Chat within the public chat room where anyone on Relay can talk in one place. Meet new people, join conversations, and stay in the loop.

Direct messages

Send direct messages to anyone for a private one to one chat. Keep conversations personal and separate from the main chat.

Group chats

Create group chats and add people to keep everything in one place. Great for friends, school, or working on something together.

Reactions and replies

React to messages with emojis or reply directly to them. It makes it easier to respond and keep conversations clear.

Image sharing

Share photos and screenshots right in the chat. Everything stays in the same place so it is easy to see and follow.

Moderation and safety

Report messages, block users, and use tools that help keep the platform safe for everyone.

Why Relay?

Built for every kind of conversation.

Relay pairs end to end encryption with clear, purposeful chat types, so you can choose the right space for the moment and trust who can read what you send.

End to end encryption

Messages in direct and group chats are encrypted on your device and decrypted only by the people you send them to.

Private chats

Start a one to one conversation with anyone on Relay and keep it focused, private, and easy to return to.

Group chats

Create a group, add members, and keep teams, classes, or circles of friends in one shared space.

Public chat

Drop into the open room where everyone on Relay can post, reply, and react together in real time.
